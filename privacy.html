Privacy Policy – YouTube Music Together

Last updated: 2025-01-01

YouTube Music Together does not collect, store, use, or share any personal or sensitive user data.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • Personal information
  • Account or authentication data
  • Financial information
  • Location data
  • Browsing history
  • User activity or keystrokes
  • Device identifiers

Technical Data Used Only for Playback Sync

The extension only exchanges minimal anonymous playback data needed to synchronize music between participants:

  • Room ID
  • Playback timestamp
  • Play/pause/track change events

This information is anonymous, temporary, and not linked to any individual.

No Data Sharing

No information is sold, shared, or transferred to third parties.

Third-Party Services

The extension uses Firebase Realtime Database solely for real-time playback synchronization. No personal data is sent or stored.

Contact

This extension does not require direct user support and does not provide a contact email because no personal data is processed.
